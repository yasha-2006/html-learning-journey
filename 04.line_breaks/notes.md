## Line Break Tag

The `<br>` tag is used to insert a line break in HTML.
It moves the content after it to the next line without starting a new paragraph.

## Syntax

```html
Line 1<br>
Line 2
```
Output:

Line 1
Line 2

Example:

```html
<p>
My name is Yasha.<br>
I am learning HTML.<br>
I love coding.
</p>

## Important Points


- `<br>` stands for Break.
- It creates a new line.
- It is an empty tag.
- It does not require a closing tag.
- It is commonly used for addresses, poems, and contact information.

## Difference Between `<p>` and `<br>`

`<p>`                              | `<br>`
 * Creates a paragraph             |*  Creates a line break
 * Adds spacing before and after   |*  No extra spacing 
 *  Has opening and closing tags   |*  Empty tag
